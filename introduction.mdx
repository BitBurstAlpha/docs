<Accordion title="Who AM I?">
  The AI Customer Assistant Chatbot with Integrated Ticketing System is a hybrid support platform that combines automated assistance with human support to deliver a seamless customer service experience. The system is designed to handle a wide range of customer queries in real-time using artificial intelligence and natural language processing.
  It can provide instant responses to frequently asked questions, guide users through troubleshooting steps, and escalate complex issues to human agents when necessary. The integrated ticketing system allows for efficient tracking and management of customer inquiries, ensuring that no request goes unanswered. This solution is ideal for businesses looking to enhance their customer support capabilities while reducing response times and improving overall satisfaction.
